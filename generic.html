About Us

Find out what makes our business different from the other guys!

Where we started.

Yggdrasil Tree Service is a christian family-owned, local small business founded by Zayne Ware and Trevor Brooks In 2021 after they had spent multiple years working for large corporate tree trimming companies, the pair realized that with hard work and dedication, they would be able to do better work for the people in their community and for much less than the corporate companies were charging. With the vision of offering their local community a more affordable and reliable source of tree services, Yggdrasil Tree Service was created and has been serving the community since.

Where we are.

Today, Yggdrasil Tree Service is known throughout the area for their excellent customer relations, great work ethic and their overall commitment to excellence. Yggdrasil Tree Service continues to grow and improve but for owners Zayne Ware and Trevor Brooks it is more about helping those in their local community by offering affordable services.

Where we are headed.

Like a tree, Yggdrasil Tree Service wants to grow. We hope that we will continue to expand and be able to assist even more people in more communities all over the Carolinas. We will maintain our local-business feel but the farther our reach becomes, the more people we will be able to help with affordable solutions to our neighbors problems. Do not be surprised when you see us in your neighborhood!
